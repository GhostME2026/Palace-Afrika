The Lodge · Pantang, Accra

Sankofa — go back and get it

A home for repatriates.
A welcome for everyone.

Palace Afrika Guest Lodge sits quietly in Pantang, West Accra, in the La Nkwantanang Madina Municipal district of Greater Accra — close enough to the city's energy, far enough for real rest. We built this lodge around a simple idea borrowed from the Adinkra symbol Sankofa: it is never wrong to go back for what you left behind.

For members of the diaspora returning home, first-time visitors chasing an authentic Ghana experience, or families passing through Accra, Palace Afrika is more than a guest house — it is a soft landing. Warm rooms, home-cooked hospitality, and a team that treats every guest like family.

  • Home for repatriates & the diaspora
  • Quiet residential setting in Pantang, Accra
  • Personal, family-run hospitality
  • Curated Ghana tours & excursions on request
